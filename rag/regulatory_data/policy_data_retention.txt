
# Visa Digital Payments - Data Retention Policy
Version 2.1 | Effective: January 2024

## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes standards for retaining customer and transaction data in compliance with PCI-DSS, GDPR, and CCPA requirements.

## 2. Cardholder Data Retention
2.1 Sensitive Authentication Data (SAD) must be deleted immediately after authorization.
2.2 Primary Account Numbers (PAN) shall be stored only when business necessity is documented.
2.3 Cardholder data retention period: Maximum 18 months unless required by law.
2.4 All stored PAN must be encrypted using AES-256 or equivalent.

## 3. Personal Data Under GDPR
3.1 Customer personal data shall be retained for no longer than necessary for the purpose collected.
3.2 Default retention: 3 years after last transaction for EU customers.
3.3 Right to erasure requests must be processed within 30 days.

## 4. CCPA Compliance
4.1 California residents' data shall not be sold without explicit opt-in consent.
4.2 Data subject access requests must be fulfilled within 45 days.
4.3 Deletion requests shall be processed within 45 days unless exception applies.
