
# Visa Digital Payments - Access Control Policy
Version 3.0 | Effective: January 2024

## 1. Purpose
Establish access controls for systems handling payment card data and personal information.

## 2. Authentication Requirements
2.1 Multi-factor authentication (MFA) must be enabled for all administrative access.
2.2 Password complexity: Minimum 12 characters, alphanumeric with special characters.
2.3 Password rotation: Every 90 days for privileged accounts.

## 3. Role-Based Access Control
3.1 Access shall be granted based on job function and least privilege principle.
3.2 Access reviews must be conducted quarterly.
3.3 Terminated employee access must be revoked within 24 hours.

## 4. Cardholder Data Environment (CDE)
4.1 CDE access shall be restricted to personnel with documented business need.
4.2 All CDE access must be logged and monitored in real-time.
4.3 Remote access to CDE requires VPN with MFA.
