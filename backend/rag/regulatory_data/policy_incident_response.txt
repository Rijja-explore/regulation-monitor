
# Visa Digital Payments - Incident Response Policy
Version 2.0 | Effective: January 2024

## 1. Purpose
Define procedures for detecting, responding to, and reporting security incidents and data breaches.

## 2. Incident Classification
2.1 Critical: Unauthorized access to cardholder data or breach of CDE
2.2 High: Suspected compromise of payment systems
2.3 Medium: Potential vulnerability exploitation attempts
2.4 Low: Policy violations without data exposure

## 3. Response Procedures
3.1 Critical incidents must be escalated to CISO within 1 hour of detection.
3.2 Forensic preservation shall begin immediately upon incident confirmation.
3.3 Affected parties must be notified within 72 hours for GDPR breaches.
3.4 Payment card brands must be notified of compromises within 24 hours.

## 4. Documentation
4.1 All incidents shall be documented with timeline, impact, and remediation steps.
4.2 Post-incident reviews must be conducted within 7 days.
